About ZeroHunger

Our mission is to work towards a world where poverty no longer exists.

The goal of ZeroHunger is to eradicate the problem of hunger and malnutrition around the world. This means ensuring that all people have access to sufficient, safe, and nutritious food to meet their dietary needs and food preferences for an active and healthy life. This is a challenging and ambitious goal, but one that is vital to the health and well-being of individuals and communities.

Working towards zero hunger involves a variety of strategies, including increasing food production, improving distribution and access to food, promoting sustainable agriculture and food systems, and supporting the development of food security policies at the local, national, and global levels. It also involves addressing the underlying causes of hunger, such as poverty, inequality, and conflict. Ultimately, achieving zero hunger requires a coordinated effort from governments, civil society, the private sector, and individuals around the world.
